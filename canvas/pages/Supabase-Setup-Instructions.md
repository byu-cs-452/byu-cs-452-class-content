**Setup your postgres instance**  
We will use Supabase as our managed postgres database service to complete the lab.  
First, create a free account with Supabase. www.Supabase.com and then set up your organization. Make sure you pick the Free option.

![image.png](../files/Uploaded%20Media/image-55be38a3-f632-4cf9-bbd9-9371984a1e98.png)

**Create a new Project**  
Give the project a name, and the database a password. You’ll want to copy this password to use it later.  
 ![image.png](../files/Uploaded%20Media/image-34144cf6-daa0-4f56-be86-71d71da777b4.png)

**Connection String**  
Click ‘Connect’ at the top.

![image.png](../files/Uploaded%20Media/image-330c7bd9-e745-40b4-91fa-de3ec340d422.png)

Copy the **Transaction Pooler** String.   
   
You will either paste this into your code, or load it into your recommendation app with an environment variable.
